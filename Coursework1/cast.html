Estelle Fanta Swaray

Estelle Fanta Swaray (born 18 January 1980), simply known as Estelle, is a British singer, songwriter and record producer from West London, England. Estelle is known for her eclectic mix of various musical genres including R&B, soul, reggae, grime, hip hop and dance.

Zach Callison

Zachry Callison (born October 23, 1997) is an American actor and singer. He is known as the voice of Steven Universe, the title character of Cartoon Network's Steven Universe and its follow-ups Steven Universe: The Movie and Steven Universe Future.

Deedee Magno Hall

Deedee Magno Hall (born April 2, 1975) is an American actress and singer. She is best known for her work in the pop group The Party, and as the voice of Pearl in the animated series Steven Universe and its epilogue series Steven Universe Future.
